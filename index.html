How to Play the Crypto Lottery

Follow these simple steps:

  1. Connect your MetaMask account.
  2. Select an account from the dropdown menu.
  3. Buy tickets by selecting the number of tickets and clicking "Buy Tickets."
  4. Wait for the manager to draw a winner.
  5. Check the results on the "Winners" page.

Enjoy a transparent and fair lottery experience powered by blockchain technology!

What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a decentralized ledger technology that securely records transactions in a chain of blocks. Each block is linked to the previous block using cryptography, making it immutable and secure.

Key Features:

  • Transparency: Transactions are publicly available but keep users anonymous.
  • Security: Cryptographic algorithms ensure the data is tamper-proof.
  • Decentralization: No single entity controls the blockchain.

The Crypto Lottery uses blockchain technology for transparent and secure lottery operations.
